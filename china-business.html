Phase 1: Pre-Licensing & Planning

Before applying, define the company's structure and secure a physical location. Virtual offices are not permitted.

  • Business Scope: Must be explicitly defined (e.g., "Engineering Design", "CAD Drafting").
  • Registered Capital: Declare an amount sufficient to cover initial operations.
  • Lease Agreement: Sign a commercial lease with valid property ownership certificates.

Phase 2: Document Legalization

Chinese authorities require authenticated proof of the foreign investor's identity.

  • US Passports or Corporate Documents (LLC/Corp) must be notarized and authenticated by the state.
  • With the Apostille Convention in effect, state-issued Apostilles streamline this previously complex consular process.

Phase 3: Business License Application

Formal registration via the "Five-in-One" online portal.

  • Submit Articles of Association and appoint the Legal Representative.
  • The Administration for Market Regulation (AMR) issues the Business License. The company now legally exists.

Phase 4: Post-Licensing & Company Chops

Physical stamps (chops) hold supreme legal weight in China.

  • Register with the Public Security Bureau to carve Official, Financial, Legal Representative, and Invoice chops.

Phase 5: Bank Accounts & Tax Registration

Finalizing operational setup to hire and transact.

  • Open an RMB Basic Account and a Foreign Currency Capital Account.
  • Register with the State Taxation Administration for "Fapiao" (VAT invoice) issuance.
  • Open social security and housing fund accounts for future employees.

Engineering Talent Hubs

Moving beyond Tier 1 cities unlocks massive geographic arbitrage. Tier 2 and 3 cities house elite technical universities, providing highly skilled drafters and modelers at a fraction of coastal costs.

Shanghai

Tier 1

Premium coastal hub. Highest English proficiency, highest costs.

Universities: Tongji Univ., SJTU

Nanjing

New Tier 1

Wealthy hub near Shanghai. Elite architectural and civil talent.

Universities: Southeast Univ. (SEU)

Wuhan

New Tier 1

Massive student population. Deep mechanical & civil CAD pools.

Universities: HUST, Wuhan Univ.

Chengdu

New Tier 1

High retention rates, exceptional lifestyle. Strong BIM/Software.

Universities: Sichuan Univ., UESTC

Tianjin

New Tier 1

Bordering Beijing. Historical excellence in chemical/civil engineering.

Universities: Tianjin Univ.

Xi'an

Tier 2

Inland aerospace and heavy engineering capital. Great cost-to-skill ratio.

Universities: XJTU, NWPU

Hefei

Tier 2

Rapidly growing tech and manufacturing hub. Highly competitive rates.

Universities: USTC, HFUT

Harbin

Tier 3

Northern industrial base. Lowest costs, legendary engineering school.

Universities: Harbin Inst. of Tech (HIT)
